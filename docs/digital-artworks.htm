The Origins.

As much as I love painting on real canvases, I still enjoy doing digital art. From creating illustrations to photoshopping weird stuff together. The digital world is still the place where I initially discovered and fell in love with the beauty of art.

At the moment I’m doing more design related stuff, mainly because of my job as a creative director. But from time to time, I grab my beloved drawing tablet and going back to the good old times where it all started.
