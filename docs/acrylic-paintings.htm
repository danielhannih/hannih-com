Discovering my Passion.

It was around 2014, and I just got the opportunity to paint a massive mural in an office space. At this time my whole world was digital. And I never thought about doing traditional work, like a painting. But this opportunity seemed like a perfect fit. After finishing the mural, I bought a couple of acrylics and canvases and finally started breaking out of this digital world. And so it all began.

Looking back, I’m thankful how the most obvious thing started to grow. A passion that was necessary to break out of this cold and digital life. Where I felt limited, staring at displays and pixels. Being able to create and express my feelings in colors and strokes, is one of the most satisfying moments in life. But after all, it will never satisfy my urge to creating more.
